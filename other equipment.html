Other equipment

Ammunition

Anti-Armor ammo/mod

Highly illegal. Any damage dealt to armor HP is doubled. Any other damage dealt by it is lowered by one.

Anti-Shield ammo/mod

Highly illegal. Any damage dealt to shield HP is doubled. Any other damage dealt by it is lowered by one.

Tracer ammo/mod

Highlights projectile trajectory. Gives +1 to hit, if fired as single shot. +2 to burst fire. +4 to heavy weapons.

Armor accessories

Armor cammo (Urban/Desert/Jungle/Marine/Plains)

Special paint that obscures details of armor. Gives +1 AC until damaged for 5 points.

Reflective cammo

Gives 2 dmg resistance against lasers until hit by any other weaponry. Outer coating is made of black paint, that becomes transparent when heated.

Implants

Shield implants (50 000 cr per level of implant, 1 system strain per level of implant)

Once per scene, user can gain 5 shield HP per level of implant. Shields act as vaccskin. If character is psionic, their effort is lowered by one per level of implant.
