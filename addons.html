Addons

External Mounts

Targeting camera (1 mount, -1 energy, +2 to hit, 100 cr)

Wrist mounted camera that helps with aiming. Doesn’t stack with targeting laser.

Targeting laser (1 mount, -1 energy, +1 to hit, 20 cr)

Wrist mounted laser that helps aiming, can be used to paint targets. Doesn’t stack with targeting camera.

Integrated melee weaponry (1 mount, -1 energy, 200 cr + weapon)

Mount that integrates weapon into armor, weapon must be bought separately, weapon adds no enc, and is always readied. If armor is powered, damage is increased by two points.

Integrated ballistic weaponry (1 mount, -1 energy, 200 cr + weapon, 1 enc)

Mount that integrates weapon into armor, weapon must be bought separately. Weapon adds no enc, is always readied and reloading it from ammo feed is an instant action.

Integrated energy weaponry (1 mount, -2 energy, 200 cr + weapon)

Mount that integrates weapon into armor, weapon must be bought separately. Weapon adds no enc, is always readied and it has infinite ammo. Firing it without separate ammo requires energy to be committed for round instead of ammo.

Integrated heavy weaponry (1 mount, -1 energy, 200 cr + weapon, 2 enc)

Mount that integrates heavy weapon into armor, weapon must be bought separately, weapon adds no enc, is always readied and reloading it is an instant action.

Power access (1 mount, 50 cr)

Can be used to access power grid of the suit while it’s being worn, and either boost it from external source, or use suit's energy to power external device/weapon.

Heat radiator (1 mount, 50 cr)

Removes one heat from system per turn. Effect stacks.

Drone dock (1 mount, 50 cr)

Makes dock for drone on the surface of armor. One drone can be recharged and carried for no enc here. Drone controller can be integrated. Effect stacks.

Holo-emitter unit (1 mount, 200 cr, -1 energy)

Can be used to project medium sized holograms. (up to half meter in all directions) Armor’s interface or communication system can be projected through this module.

Sensoric Slots

IR vision (1 slot, 150 cr)

Feeds adjustable overlay into HUD. Shows heat traces and signatures.

Low-light vision (1 slot, low light goggles and 10 cr)

Removes any penalty caused by low light.

Tactical information link (1 slot, 500 cr)

Scans surrounding and provides user with information about objects of interests if connected to data grid with available data on the subject. It can, for example, provide personal information of person in front of user, or specs or at least frame type of any perceived armor. Allows for targeting subsystem, if enemy armor isn’t shielded from sensors.

Battlefield evaluation sensor (1 slot, 500 cr, -1 energy, +2 to hit)

Provides useful information about battlefield. It helps to aim, giving +2 bonus to hit. And ability to see which targets are painted. Allows for targeting subsystem, if enemy armor isn’t shielded from sensors. Gives basic IFF.

Optimized control HUD (1 slot, 200 cr)

This addon expands and optimizes default Armor management HUD, allowing single Manage armor action to manage multiple addons.

Auxiliary Systems

Sound suppressor (1 slot, 200 cr, -1 energy)

Creates field in which all sound is dampened. It can dampen all sound created by armor for one heat per turn, or all sound in 10m sphere for 4 heat per turn.

First aid kit (1 slot, 500 cr, -1 energy)

Stabilizes mortally wounded user. Maximizes heat in the suit, creates at least five heat. When one energy is committed for the turn can be used on person outside armor.
